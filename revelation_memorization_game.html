Revelation 3:14–22 Memorization Game

Game: Verse Ladder Challenge • Players: 1–6 • Time: 15–25 mins

🎯 Goal: Memorize verses 14–22 exactly 🏁 Highest total score wins 🧠 Use cards + checker answer sheet

Setup

  1. Copy the exact verse cards below.
  2. Cut each verse into separate cards (14–22).
  3. Shuffle cards and place them face down.
  4. Keep one full-text answer sheet visible only to the checker.

How to Play (Rounds)

  1. Round 1 — Order Race: Arrange all 9 cards in order (14→22). First correct sequence = 2 points.
  2. Round 2 — Speak & Flip: Flip one verse, recite exactly. Exact = 2, one mistake = 1, 2+ mistakes = 0.
  3. Round 3 — Neighbor Chain: Recite drawn verse + previous + next verse. Exact all three = 3 points.
  4. Round 4 — Full Passage Sprint: Recite verses 14–22 in order. Perfect = 10, 1–2 errors = 8, 3–5 errors = 6.
