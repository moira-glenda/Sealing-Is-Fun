# Revelation 3:14–22 Memorization Game (Exact Text)

## Game: **Verse Ladder Challenge**

### Goal
Memorize Revelation 3:14–22 exactly as shown.

### Players
1–6

### Time
15–25 minutes per round

---

## Setup
1. Copy the **Exact Verse Cards** section below.
2. Cut each verse into separate cards (14 through 22).
3. Shuffle cards and place them face down.
4. Keep one answer sheet with the full text visible only to the checker.

---

## How to Play

### Round 1 — Order Race
- Each player draws all 9 cards and arranges them in correct verse order (14 to 22).
- First correct sequence gets **2 points**.

### Round 2 — Speak & Flip
- Top card is turned over (for example, verse 17).
- Player must recite that verse exactly.
- Checker compares to answer sheet.
- Exact recitation = **2 points**.
- One mistake = **1 point**.
- Two or more mistakes = **0 points**.

### Round 3 — Neighbor Chain
- Player draws one card and must recite:
  1) that verse,
  2) the previous verse number text,
  3) the next verse number text.
- Exact all three = **3 points**.

### Round 4 — Full Passage Sprint
- Recite from verse 14 to verse 22 in order.
- Scoring:
  - Perfect = **10 points**
  - 1–2 word errors total = **8 points**
  - 3–5 word errors total = **6 points**

Highest score wins.

## Exact Verse Cards (No Modification)

### 14
"To the angel of the church in Laodicea write:
These are the words of the Amen, the faithful and true witness,
the ruler of God's creation.

### 15
I know your deeds, that you are neither cold nor hot.
I wish you were either one or the other!

### 16
So, because you are lukewarm-neither hot nor cold-
I am about to spit you out of my mouth.

### 17
You say, 'I am rich; I have acquired wealth and do not need a thing.'
But you do not realize that you are wretched, pitiful, poor, blind
and naked.

### 18
I counsel you to buy from me gold refined in the fire,
so you can become rich; and white clothes to wear,
so you can cover your shameful nakedness;
and salve to put on your eyes, so you can see.

### 19
Those whom I love I rebuke and discipline.
So be earnest, and repent.

### 20
Here I am! I stand at the door and knock.
If anyone hears my voice and opens the door,
I will come in and eat with him, and he with me.

### 21
To him who overcomes,
I will give the right to sit with me on my throne,
just as I overcame and sat down with my Father on his throne.

### 22
He who has an ear,
let him hear what the Spirit says to the churches."

## Optional Difficulty Modes
- **Lightning (Hard):** 10-second timer per verse.
- **Numberless Mode:** Hide verse numbers; players identify the number before reciting.
- **Team Relay:** Each teammate recites one verse in sequence without pauses.
